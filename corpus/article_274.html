Schools in England: share your thoughts on the proposal to ban mobile phones

Publication Date: 2023-07-25

Author: Guardian community team

Section: Technology

Tags: Smartphones, Mobile phones, Schools, callout

Article URL: https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2023/jul/25/teachers-share-your-experiences-of-students-using-smartphones-in-school

Mobile phones could be banned from schools in England, if guidance proposed by the Conservative education secretary Gillian Keegan is successful. She is reportedly due to announce the guidance to the Tory party conference in Manchester on Monday. A government source told the Daily Mail that Keegan believes mobile phones “pose a serious challenge in terms of distraction” We would like to hear from teachers and parents in England about their thoughts on the proposal to ban mobile phones. How does your school manage usage at the moment? Do you have any concerns? This Community callout closed on 15 November 2023. You can see the article that included respondents to this callout here.
