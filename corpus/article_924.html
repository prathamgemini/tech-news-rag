Tell us: have you experienced last-minute cancellations on Airbnb?

Publication Date: 2024-04-18

Author: Guardian community team

Section: Technology

Tags: Airbnb, Taylor Swift, callout

Article URL: https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2024/apr/18/tell-us-have-you-experiences-last-minute-cancellations-on-airbnb

We’re interested in hearing from people who have made a booking for accommodation on an online travel platform, only for their host to cancel it at the last minute. Earlier this year, Taylor Swift fans in Australia were left without accommodation after Airbnb hosts cancelled their bookings. Airbnb’s “host cancellation policy” does not seem to have deterred hosts from then relisting their properties at a higher price. Has something similar happened to you? Are you a Swiftie whose plans to watch her European tour have been affected? Or have you made plans for a holiday elsewhere or another trip that have been thrown into disarray by a host’s cancellation?
